What We Stand For

01

Vision

To be the most influential cultural art hub in Iraq and empower the sense of artistic expression in the youth.

History

How It Started

Founded in 2016 by Mohammed Sabah, the club ran for two years screening over 30 films — each with an introduction and a Q&A session that sparked real dialogue.

Now it's back. Stronger. With a bigger vision for what cinema can do for Basra.
